About The Event

Artificial intelligence (AI) has become an inevitable part in our daily life. In this era where AI is gradually making its presence in every aspect of common man's life, computer science researchers are responsible to design and develop AI enabled products that are safe, reliable and ethical. Through this seminar we try to introduce the notion of developing efficient algorithms as well as the mathematical perspective of AI. Besides this, the seminar attempts to provide an insight about NLP and role of AI in Network Security. The seminar also unveils the upcoming trends in AI research. This seminar would act as a platform for researchers in AI across India to meet and explore possibilities for collaborative research. We envision to create an opportunity for the students and researchers to interact with eminent resource persons from academia as well as industry.

10:45 - 12:00PM Session

Machine Learning Algorithms For Network Security

Dr. Remya Krishnan P

Delve into the pragmatic realm of cybersecurity with a focus on 'Machine Learning Algorithms for Network Security.' This session offers a systematic exploration of the application of machine learning in bolstering digital network defenses. Attendees can expect to gain insights into practical strategies and algorithms that contribute to the enhancement of network security. The session is designed to provide a formal and informative examination of this crucial intersection between machine learning and cybersecurity

Location: Seminar Hall
12:15 - 1:30PM Session

Introduction to Explainable AI

Dr. Manu Madhavan

Explore the realm of Explainable AI (XAI), where clarity intersects with artificial intelligence. This session focuses on demystifying the decision-making processes of AI algorithms, providing a formal exploration of how conclusions are reached. Through a clear and concise lens, we aim to elucidate the inner workings of AI models, fostering a transparent understanding of their outputs. Join us as we navigate the landscape of Explainable AI, bridging the gap between the intricacies of machine learning and the necessity for comprehensible decision outputs.

Location: Seminar Hall
2:30 - 3:30PM Session

Unveiling the world of generative ai

Mr. Nikhil Dev

Exploring Generative AI: Join us for an in-depth session delving into the world of Generative AI. This session will unveil the capabilities and applications of Generative AI, providing insights into how algorithms can dynamically generate innovative content. Gain a deeper understanding of the potential of Generative AI and its impact on various domains. Don't miss this session as we navigate through the fascinating landscape of Generative AI together.

Location: Seminar Hall

An introduction to nlp and computational linguistics

Discover the essentials of NLP and computational linguistics in this session. Gain insights into the fundamental principles that underpin Natural Language Processing and its synergy with computational linguistics. Whether you are a novice or aiming to deepen your understanding, this session provides valuable insights into the foundations and applications of NLP.

This session offers a comprehensive view of the mathematical underpinnings that empower AI algorithms, providing participants with valuable insights into the quantitative aspects shaping the field.

Explore the art of crafting efficient algorithms in the era of AI. This session delves into the principles and methodologies behind designing algorithms that optimize performance within the dynamic landscape of artificial intelligence. Gain insights into the strategies that contribute to the efficiency and effectiveness of algorithms in the evolving field of AI.

About Our College

Kodiyeri Balakrishnan Memorial Government College Thalassery was launched under the one college for one constituency scheme initiated by Govt. of Kerala in the year 2014. The college was established utilizing the asset development fund of the then MLA Late Sri Kodiyeri Balakrishnan who was instrumental in setting up the college. The college is situated in a picturesque and tranquil locality, Olavilam which lies near the Mayyazhi river. The college has been receiving immense support from the general public and the main campus is setup in around 6 acres of land acquired through public initiative at Thuluvar hills, in Kanhirathinkeezhil of Chokli Panchayath.
